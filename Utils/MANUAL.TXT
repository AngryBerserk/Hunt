{-=MONSTERS=-}

AGRESSIVE MONSTERS ARE ABLE TO RANDOMLY DISABLE SCANNERS,
STEALTH MONSTER ARE HIDING FROM YOUR VIEW ANDABLE TO RANDOMLY AVOID LANDMINES,
COWARD MONSTERS CAN GO BERSERK AND DO DOUBLE DAMAGE IF LOCATED.
MONSTERS DAMAGE,HEALTH,BERSERK LEVEL, ABILITY TO DISABLE SCANNERS,
SPEED AND ABILITY TO AVOID LANDMINES DEPENDS ON DIFFICULTY.

{-=WEAPONS=-}

WEAPONS ACCURACY SUFFERS RANGE AND DIFFICULTY PENALTY.
SOME WEAPONS CAN MAKE A MULTIPLY SHOTS PER MOVE. EACH SHOT IS CALCULATED
INDIVIDUALY.
DAMAGE LEVEL NOT DEPENDS ON RANGE AND DIFFICULTY.
